About Sela's Creations

Based in Australia, Sela's Creations is led by a screenwriter and programmer, bringing technical problem-solving together with character-driven storytelling.

The screenwriting work focuses on serialized drama, emotional arcs, world-building, and long-form narrative design.

The technical work centres on tools that support creative workflows, including custom databases and writing systems.
